## ğŸ¨ Design Philosophy

I based version 1u on three principles:

### 1. Information Density
> "Show more, scroll less"

- Compact cards maximize visible activities
- 5-column layout shows all categories at once
- Level splits (L2|L3) provide organization

### 2. Direct Manipulation
> "Touch it, move it, done"

- Drag-and-drop is most direct way to assign
- Click to view is more natural than buttons
- Hover to reveal actions reduces clutter

### 3. Progressive Disclosure
> "Details on demand"

- Compact cards show essentials
- Modal reveals full information
- Tabs organize complex content
- Tooltips provide help
